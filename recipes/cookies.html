"Keto Cookies"

When making food more keto-friendly... things tend to get interesting. The coconut flour is fluffy and has a nice sweet smell and taste, while mozzarella add a lot to the chew and you can never have too much yogurt. Theses are one of many attempts at making chewy cookies with minimum carbs from a culmination of recipes I've tried online.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut flour
  • 1/4 shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp erythritol or another sweetener
  • 3 tbsp sugar-free chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven at 325 F or 300 F with an convection oven.
  2. Add butter and sweetener to a bowl and cream with a handmixer until smooth.
  3. Microwave mozzarella for 30 seconds to melt then immediately mix with the creamed butter.
  4. Add in yogurt, vanilla extract and mix well.
  5. In another bowl, mix all the dry ingredients together first then combine with wet until dough is formed.
  6. Form into balls and press into cookie shapes on baking tray.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes on the middle rack.
  8. Let it cool down on a wire and enjoy!
